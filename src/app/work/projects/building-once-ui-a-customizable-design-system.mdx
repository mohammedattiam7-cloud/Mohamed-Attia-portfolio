## The bet

Funded, pre-launch Restaurant OS for MENA. The founders' rule: product-led, not sales-led. No sales team — so the design had to sell the product itself. My job: make the product prove its value alone, in the first hour.

## Six roles. One screen. Opposite needs.

The cashier wants speed. The kitchen wants detail. The manager wants control. Every design choice makes one of them slow.
