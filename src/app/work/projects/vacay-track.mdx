## Product Context

Vacay Track is a mobile employee self-service app for managing leave, attendance, requests, and daily tasks in one place. Before it, every action lived in a different tool — a leave request meant an email, attendance lived in a sheet, and an employee had no clear view of their own status or history. The challenge was less about features and more about structure: pulling fragmented HR actions into one flow simple enough to use in a few taps, with the right status and feedback at every step.

## Problem

Leave is requested over email or paper. Tasks live in a separate work tool. Attendance and personal data sit in a third HR system. There is no single place that answers the everyday questions an employee actually has:

This fragmentation creates friction on both sides: employees feel uncertain and have to chase HR for basic answers, while HR drowns in repetitive requests routed through email.

The gap I designed against: work-management tools see the employee as a resource that executes tasks. HR tools see them as a record to administer. Neither sees the employee as a whole person who has tasks and time off and a need to know where they stand. Vacay Track was designed to close that gap.
